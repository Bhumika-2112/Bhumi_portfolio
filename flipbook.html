Page 1 — The Whisper

The wind whispered across the page, Carrying words once locked in cage. In silence loud, the ink did speak, Of dreams held high, and hearts grown weak.

Page 2 — Bloom

She bloomed not because the world was kind, But because her soul refused to stay blind. In shadows deep she found her way, And grew toward her light each day.

Page 3 — The End

Some endings are poems too, Not in words, but in the view. Close the book but leave the part, That still lives inside your heart.
