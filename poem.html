Those Brown Eyes
There was a boy with eyes of brown, His well-set hair never let him down. He walked with grace, and spoke with charm, Radiating warmth like a cozy hearth's warmth. His eyes were deep like a forest glen, And his hair was silky, like satin blend. He wandered about with a gentle grace, His presence was calming, like a warm embrace. In his eyes, you could see a spark, A twinkle that lit up in the dark. kind and gentle, this boy with brown, And his hair was magnificent; a royal crown. His smile was infectious, and his laugh so pure, He cheered up the sad, and made them feel secure. This boy with eyes of brown and well-set hair, Was a joy to behold, a treasure so rare.
