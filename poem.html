Whispers of Chaos
In tangled thoughts, the silence screams, A palette spills in shattered dreams. I sketch the storm behind calm eyes, Art born of truth no mask belies.
Echo
I spoke to the dark, and it answered me, In rhymes of ruin and melody. It knew my name, it knew my pain, And echoed it back like a soft refrain.
Ink & Soul
Each word I bleed on paper pale, A silent scream, a quiet tale. I am the poet and the poem too, A page of chaos, written true.
